History-La Nostra Storia

In 1992, Rochestarian Giuseppe Testaverde visited Avellino, Italy the homeland of his family. During his time in Avellino he was able to sample many of the delicious, traditional food offerings but what impacted him most of all were the local pizzerias. Each one served a variety of traditional Neapolitan pizzas and each with their own special touch. is favorite turned out to be one owned by a second cousin Carlo Cucina. The visit with his cousin proved to be life changing! Giuseppe decided to stay with Carlo and his family to learn the art of pizza making... the Napolitan way! With Carlo's undying love and support, Giuseppe ventured off to Brooklyn, NY to begin his own traditional pizzeria... A' Pizza.

Le Pizze

Our authentic pizza is crafted in the tradition of Naples, Italy, the birthplace of pizza. We adhere to the standards set forth by the Associazione Verace Pizza Napoletana, using fresh ingredients. We use fresh imported Italian bufala mozzarella, our sauce is created with Italian San Marzano tomatoes, and our freshly prepared hand-pressed dough creates the authentic pliant crust that made Neapolitan pizza famous.
